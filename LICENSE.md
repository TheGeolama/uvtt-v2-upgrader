### 💡 Open-Source Tooling Note (Non-Normative)

The **UVTT v2 Upgrader Web App** (including its Svelte interface, interactive PixiJS WebGPU rendering canvas, and native cryptographic signing layers) is provided as open-source reference tooling. 

* **Hosting Custom Variants:** You are fully permitted to host your own compiled instances of the Upgrader (for instance, on your own website, corporate portals, or Patreon creator dashboards) to help your community migrate legacy V1 files to the V2 standard.
* **Feature Integration:** You may extract GUI components or utility scripts (such as the in-memory 512-byte chunked base64 parser, Catmull-Rom curve smoother, or WebGPU memory scrubbers) and port them directly into your proprietary software suites.
